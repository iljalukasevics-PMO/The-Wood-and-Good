Table Tops

The Oak has long been a symbol of strength, resilience, and timeless elegance. We offer you the opportunity to integrate this solid oak masterpiece into your daily space, elevating your interior to the next level. No two tables are the same—each tabletop features a distinct, unrepeatable grain pattern.

Premium Coating

Oak Table Tops (Lacquer)

Whether it’s for focused work, relaxation, social gatherings, or home celebrations, this solid oak table is built to last and will never cease to impress. The surface is coated twice with a high-durability, two-component gloss lacquer. Side edges of the tables can be made in two styles—live (natural curvature of the tree) or straight.

Quality Descriptions

Prime — No knots, no sapwood, staves sorted by colors, staves width 50-120mm.
Character — Only sound knots, cracks filled with black filler, no sapwood, staves sorted by colors. (For Character wide all staves are >250mm wide).
Rustic — Knots without limits, lines of sapwood, cracks filled with black filler, wood discolorations, no color sorting.

Natural Protection

Oak Table Tops (Wax)

Crafted with precision and care, our solid oak table tops guarantee the utmost quality and durability. All table tops are professionally sanded and treated with an initial base layer of wax. To ensure maximum protection and the perfect finish for daily use, a final coat of wax should be applied once the table is in its permanent home—ensuring long-term protection for your investment.

Architectural Features

40mm Stability Thickness: Ensures a lifetime of stability and an organic high-end look.

Artful Wax Fills: Naturally occurring knots and cracks are artfully filled with hard black wax, making every piece a one-of-a-kind work of art.

Custom Dimensions: We offer standard sizes, however, all products can be custom-produced to your exact architectural space requirements.
